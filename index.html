About JobShop®

JobShop® is the first and the longest-lived career event ever organized in Cluj-Napoca. This year the event is reaching it's XXVIIIth edition, thus marking 28 years since JobShop® represents a bridge between companies in search of workforce and students pursuing technical education.

Location

Our partner companies are yours to explore! Come visit their stands on 10 and 11 March 2022. The location: BT Arena of Cluj-Napoca. Don't forget to bring with you:

  • Enthusiasm towards your future prospects
  • Questions for your favorite company's representatives
  • The motivation to discover new opportunities
  • Friends

The JobShop® experience awaits you! Let it shed light on your future.

FREQUENTIS

The Frequentis Group is an international supplier of communication and information systems for control centres with safety-critical tasks. These ‘Control Centre Solutions’ are developed and distributed by Frequentis in the business segments Air Traffic Management (civil and military air traffic control, aeronautical information management, air defence) and Public Safety & Transport (police, fire and rescue services, emergency medical services, vessel traffic and railways). With headquarters in Vienna, the company employs more than 1900 people worldwide, with subsidiaries, regional offices and representatives in over 50 countries. In Cluj we started in 2009 and our local team has currently 140+ colleagues and is growing.

GRAB

Grab is a technology company headquartered in Singapore with an extensive presence across Southeast Asia as its leading super app. Our R&D centers are located across the world, including China, the US, and recently in Romania. We started to write our history in Romania in January 2020 when we opened the Cluj Office R&D Centre. One of our main goals in 2022 is to grow the Cluj Office R&D Centre, implementing a clear expansion strategy in order to constantly develop in 3 key aspects: the knowledge of our team, the refinement of our techniques that we use, and the technologies for our products.
Another important objective for us is bringing together valuable professionals, people with strong values that align with our company principles, in order to improve communities and drive sustainable change. Culture fit is a key hiring criterion for us at Grab. This is to help find employees who aren’t only great at the work they do but align with the principles of the company. We trust that they will be great ambassadors for Grab while doing the best work of their lives. We are not just a company of problem solvers, we are a company of people who solve problems because we love serving people. Our main purpose is to create empowered teams and provide for our people the appropriate context to embrace challenge and diversity in the workplace. To ensure that we build that kind of organization, we have the 4Hs that define our amazing culture:

  • Heart – We love serving people and bringing added value to our customers
  • Hunger - We are resilient, adaptable, and have the tenacity to stick to our goals
  • Honor – We value integrity and the trust placed in us by our partners and customers
  • Humility – We are aware of both: strengths and weaknesses at the same time, therefore we are so eager to improve and deliver high-quality results for our customers.

Analog Devices

Analog Devices (ADI) is a global leader in the design and manufacturing of analog, mixed signal, and DSP integrated circuits to help solve the toughest engineering challenges. Having design centers all over the world, ADI is looking to attract and grow engineering talent to work on developing best in-class solutions for the industrial, consumer, automotive and communication markets. The ADI design center in Cluj focuses on system level solutions comprising hardware and software, targeted for applications such as Software Defined Radio, Instrumentation, Machine vision or Audio & Video processing. The local team is split into a few functional groups covering hardware design, FPGA/HDL design, embedded software (both bare metal and Linux), application level software (C++, Pyhton) and algorithms (MATLAB). Having all these capabilities in one place enables us to create complete reference designs and products incorporating the latest Analog Devices technologies as well as solutions from partner companies such as Intel, Xilinx, Nvidia, NXP, Microsoft or the MathWorks. The office is located in one of the newest and most modern buildings in the city, in close proximity to Iulius Mall.

BareByte

Our company provides custom software development services, in close cooperation with our clients. Currently we are focused on providing software development services for an international energy asset management company that provides physical and financial optimization of renewable and conventional energy assets, operating on energy markets in Europe.
Our developers are working as part of different teams, integrated with teams from our client, to form agile international teams. Each team is working on a variety of projects, of different types and sizes, depending on the specific domain they are focused on. They are all bound together by the technologies that they use, such as .NET Core, .NET Framework, C#, ASP.NET WebApi, WPF, SQL Server, Jira, Jenkins, Octopus Deploy, GitLab, etc.
