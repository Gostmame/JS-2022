About JobShop®

JobShop® is the first and the longest-lived career event ever organized in Cluj-Napoca. This year the event is reaching it's XXVIIIth edition, thus marking 28 years since JobShop® represents a bridge between companies in search of workforce and students pursuing technical education.

Where

Our partner companies are yours to explore! Come visit their stands on 10 and 11 March 2022. The location: BT Arena of Cluj-Napoca. Don't forget to bring with you:

  • Enthusiasm towards your future prospects
  • Questions for your favorite company's representatives
  • The motivation to discover new opportunities
  • Friends

The JobShop® experience awaits you! Let it shed light on your future.
